Frequently Asked questions

Is there any membership fee?

No, we don't charge any memebership fee.

How can I be the part of the learning team?

After first-year students learn about functions in their C Programming course, we organize a selection contest. The highest performers in this contest are then invited to join the program.

What we teach?

We tach all the advance and complex topics for the coming 2nd year students to ace in their competitive worlds and in their competitive programming.

How can I find out about events before they happen?

Following us on our social media handles is the best way to stay updated!
